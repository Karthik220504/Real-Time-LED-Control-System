Overview
This Arduino project controls two LEDs with two buttons. 
Button 1 toggles the blink rate between 1 Hz and 2 Hz. 
Button 2 switches the active LED between red and green.

Components
Arduino board
Red LED
Green LED
2 resistors (220Ω)
2 push buttons
2 pull-down resistors (10kΩ)
